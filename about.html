About Tavern & Board

We create immersive, story-rich experiences set in the world of Varethyn—where every decision has weight and every game reveals deeper lore.

Humble Beginnings

What started as a homebrew campaign evolved into a living world—one that now spans games, maps, lore, and real-world experiences. Our founders built Tavern & Board to make that world real.

Immersive Storytelling

Every guest becomes a character in a larger saga. Our venues are more than spaces—they're stages for epic choices, hidden paths, and long-forgotten secrets waiting to be uncovered.

Building Varethyn

Varethyn is our shared universe—eight realms, countless factions, magical paths, and a thousand unanswered questions. And it’s still growing, one experience at a time.
